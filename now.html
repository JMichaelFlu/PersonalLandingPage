What I'm Doing Now

This is a "now page" - a snapshot of what I'm currently focused on in my life. If we were sitting at a bar catching up, this is what I'd tell you about.

Currently Working On

  • Digital Garden: Cultivating my public second brain with notes on PKM, neurodivergence, and learning in public
  • Academic Support: Running Longhand Compass, helping neurodivergent students navigate college and develop their own learning systems
  • Civic Engagement: Working with Get Loud! Arkansas on voter registration and rights education
  • This Website: Continuing to refine my personal corner of the internet with neon bar aesthetics and honest storytelling

Current Reading & Learning

  • Exploring personal knowledge management (PKM) systems and note-taking methodologies
  • Diving deeper into web development and design principles
  • Learning about community organizing and civic engagement strategies
  • Reading about neurodivergence, ADHD, and building systems that work with my brain instead of against it

Life Updates

I'm in Little Rock, Arkansas, trying to figure out what a fulfilling life looks like when you're a late bloomer who took the scenic route. I'm building things that might go nowhere but doing it anyway, learning in public, and trying to become someone 10-year-old me would be proud of.

I'm also working on being more consistent with my writing and note-taking, showing up for my community, and creating spaces where other neurodivergent folks can feel seen and supported.
