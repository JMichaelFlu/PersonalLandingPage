COLOPHON

How this site was made, why it looks like 1994, and who to blame

🧱 The Build

Hand-written HTML5, CSS3, and vanilla JavaScript. The way the old gods intended.

Hosted on NixiHost with Cloudflare running the DNS and such. Claude Code helped me assemble the code Claude Code.

🎨 The Look

Pure 90s nostalgia: neon signs, spray paint, and Memphis-design geometry. Think blacktop after dark,the floor at the skating rink, a ghetto blaster somewhere in the distance.

  • Electric Pink
  • Cyan Burner
  • Radioactive Lime
  • Cab Yellow
  • Traffic Cone
  • Grape Soda

🔤 The Fonts

Bungee and Bungee Shade for the big loud stuff, Permanent Marker for the tags, and Space Grotesk for everything you actually have to read. All served by Google Fonts.

♿ The Principles

Fun shouldn't cost anyone access. Every animation on this site respects prefers-reduced-motion, every page has a skip link, and the markup is semantic HTML underneath all the spray paint.

🌱 Everything Else

The living, growing stuff — notes, essays, half-formed ideas — lives in the digital garden, powered by Obsidian. This site is just the front door.
