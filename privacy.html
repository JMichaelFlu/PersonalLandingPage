🔍 The Short Version

This is a one-person linktree, not a business. I don't have accounts, forms, or anything to sell you. The only things that watch you here are standard analytics — no ad networks, no trackers hawking your data to third parties.

📊 What Gets Collected

Analytics

This site runs Google Tag Manager and Google Analytics (GA4) to see roughly how many people visit and which pages they land on. Google sets cookies and collects standard analytics data (pages viewed, approximate location, device/browser type) under its own privacy policy.

The Hit Counter

The retro odometer on the homepage counts your visits using a number stored in your browser's localStorage. It never leaves your device and I can't see it — it's just there to be charming.

Everything Else

No forms, no newsletter, no accounts, no cookies beyond what Google sets for analytics. If you email me, that's a regular email — Google (or whoever hosts your inbox) handles it same as any other.

🍪 Cookies

The analytics cookies mentioned above are the only ones this site sets. You can block or clear them anytime in your browser settings, or use an extension like Google's opt-out add-on — the site works exactly the same either way.

🔗 Other Sites

Links here point out to places like the digital garden, Goodreads, and various social platforms. Once you click through, you're on their turf and covered by their privacy policy, not this one.
